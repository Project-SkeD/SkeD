About Us

It is skills of individuals that add value to organisations globally. Yet, organisations continue to hire candidates on the basis of academic qualifications, degrees and resumes - across white collar and blue collar roles. As a result, there is often a mismatch between the job requirements and the skill set of an individual.

SkeD is Asia's first job portal that enables employers to hire people based on their specific and certified skill sets. While it helps the employers to find appropriate candidates for their organisations, it also helps the job seekers with a level playing field based on skills.

SkeD also steps in to verify candidates through Aadhar before they join any organisation, thereby solving a major challenge for organisations especially in the blue collared roles. The final result is that of a skilled, certified and verified candidate for the employer!
